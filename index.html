Who We Are

The Machine Learning Mavericks are the official AIML club of JBIET. We are a passionate community of innovators, thinkers, and builders dedicated to exploring the vast universe of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Our mission is to foster a dynamic environment where students can learn, collaborate, and create cutting-edge technology. We bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application.
